Tacos

A very popular culinary preparation from Mexico
that consists of a tortilla, made of corn or wheat,
commonly rolled to contain various ingredients and some type of sauce and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or chicken (or any protein of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can (14 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed (optional)

    • For assembling tacos:

  • 12-16 small tortillas
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, sliced jalapeños, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, lime wedges

Now let's prepare it!

  1. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Add chopped onion and sauté until it becomes translucent. Then add minced garlic and cook for another minute.

  3. Add the ground beef or chicken to the skillet. Break it up with a spoon and cook until it's browned and cooked through.

  4. Stir in the chili powder, ground cumin, paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine the spices with the meat. If using black beans, add them to the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.

  5. While the filling is cooking, warm the tortillas. You can heat them on a skillet for about 30 seconds per side or wrap them in a damp paper towel and microwave for 20-30 seconds until warm and pliable.

  6. Prepare your taco toppings and set them out in bowls for easy assembly.

  7. Once the filling is ready, assemble the tacos: spoon the meat mixture onto each tortilla.

  8. Top the tacos with your desired toppings, such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, sliced jalapeños, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, and a squeeze of lime juice.

  9. Repeat until all tortillas are filled and topped.

  10. Serve the tacos immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade Mexican tacos!
