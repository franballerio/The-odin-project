Lasagna

Is a type of pasta.
Usually served in overlapping sheets
interspersed with layers of ingredients to taste,
most frequently meat in bolognese sauce
and bechamel. Its origin is Italian and the dish
is usually prepared with meat.

    Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

    Now let's prepare it!

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  3. In a large skillet or saucepan, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until the onion is translucent.

  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. Set aside.

  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with one beaten egg. Mix until well combined.

  6. Assemble the lasagna: In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of lasagna noodles on top, slightly overlapping. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Add another layer of meat sauce.

  7. Repeat the layers: noodles, remaining ricotta mixture, mozzarella, Parmesan, and meat sauce. Top with a final layer of noodles and the remaining meat sauce. Sprinkle the top generously with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.

  9. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.

  10. Once baked, allow the lasagna to cool for about 10-15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired. Serve and enjoy!
